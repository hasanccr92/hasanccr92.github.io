About Me

Hello, I am Rakib!

I am a Computer Science student with a passion for AI, ML, NLP, and Pattern Recognition. Currently, I am a student at UPC enrolled in Erasmus Mundus Joint Master's Degree in Communications Engineering and Data Science(CoDaS). Previously I have worked as an adjunct lecturer at BRAC University and gained valuable hands-on experience as a Software Development Intern at Megamind Tech, where I actively engaged with state-of-the-art ML and AI technologies. Besides, I was a dedicated student tutor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering and the Department of Mathematics and Natural Sciences at BRAC University. My research works focus on a wide range of applications of Artificial Intelligence. My vision is to solve problems and design cost-effective and practical solutions for social welfare and sustainability utilizing cutting-edge technologies. I have authored several conference papers and presented my research papers. In addition, I have actively participated as a reviewer in the peer-review process of research papers in different high-impact journals. Outside of my academic interests, I love to play and watch football, watch TV shows and movies and listen to music.
