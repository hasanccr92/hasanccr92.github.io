About Rakib

S. M. Rakib Hasan is inquisitive, vibrant, and earnest in his desire to live life to the fullest. He loves and cherishes his family & friends. Besides, he is honest in his job and completes everything on schedule striving for perfection. He enjoys teaching, spending time with family & friends and travels whenever he gets a break.

Family

Rakib is blessed to have a very jolly and peaceful family. He is immensely fortunate to have both his parents. His father is a retired Professor of a medical college and mother is serving as the Principal of a Degree college. They are both highly qualified in academic, social and humanitarian aspects. He has an elder brother who serves as a Government Official having completed his BSc with honours in EEE from RUET.
